# Design System Quick Start for Admins & Volunteers

## 🚀 Change Your Website Design in 30 Seconds

### Step 1: Open Design Presets
1. Log in to the **Admin Panel**
2. Click **"🎨 Design Presets"** in the left menu
3. See 4 beautiful color themes

### Step 2: Pick Your Theme
- **Blue & Green** - Professional, green-focused (Default)
- **Modern Dark** - Dark, sophisticated  
- **Warm Orange** - Friendly, energetic
- **Ocean Blue** - Calming, trustworthy

### Step 3: Click "Apply"
That's it! Your entire website now uses the new theme.

## 🔄 Reset to Default

If something goes wrong or you want to start fresh:

1. Go to **Website Builder CMS**
2. Scroll down to **"Restore Default Theme"**
3. Click the button
4. All colors reset to the beautiful original design

## 💡 Pro Tips

### Tip 1: Use Presets First
Start with a preset theme, then tweak individual colors if needed.

### Tip 2: Test on Mobile
After changing colors, view the site on mobile to ensure good contrast and readability.

### Tip 3: Backup First
If making big changes, note down your current colors so you can revert if needed.

### Tip 4: Color Contrast
Make sure your text color has enough contrast with the background for accessibility.

### Tip 5: Logo Sizing
When uploading a new logo, use a square image (500x500px or similar) for best results.

## ❓ Common Questions

**Q: Do I need to know HTML/CSS?**
A: No! Everything is point-and-click in the admin panel.

**Q: Will design changes affect volunteers?**
A: No, volunteers can still do their work. Design changes are instant and global.

**Q: Can I have different designs for different pages?**
A: Yes! There's an "Events Page Override" setting for custom page backgrounds.

**Q: What if I mess up?**
A: Click "Reset Default Colors" to restore the original beautiful design.

**Q: Do I need to copy files to other servers?**
A: No! The design is in the database. As long as you backup your database, your design travels with your data.
